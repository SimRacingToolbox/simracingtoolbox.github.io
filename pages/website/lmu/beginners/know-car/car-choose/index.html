Best Cars To Choose

Best Starter Class

LMGT3 cars are the best starting point in Le Mans Ultimate.
They're perfect for learning racing lines, braking points, and traffic handling in multiclass races

Start with these cars:

BMW M4 LMGT3

Name: BMW M4 LMGT3
Class: LMGT3
Manufacturer: BMW
Engine: V8 Twin Turbo – Front Engine

The BMW M4 GT3 is the ideal choice for newcomers to LMU. Its stable handling and forgiving nature make it easy to control, even in tricky situations. It’s great for learning braking points, cornering, and overall race consistency without being too demanding. A perfect starting point for building your skills.

MCLAREN 720s

Name: McLaren 720s
Class: LMGT3
Manufacturer: McLaren
Engine: V8 Twin Turbo – Mid Engine

The McLaren 720S GT3 is a more agile option that suits beginners ready to progress. Its mid-engine layout offers sharp handling and quick direction changes, rewarding smooth driving. It’s slightly more challenging than the BMW, but a great pick once you’re confident and want more performance and precision.

PORSCHE 963

Name: Porsche 963
Class: Hypercar
Manufacturer: Porsche
Engine: V8 Twin Turbo – Mid Engine

The Porsche 963 is a balanced Hypercar ideal for beginners wanting to try the top class. It offers stable handling and good control at high speeds, making it easier to manage than many other Hypercars. With practice, it rewards smooth driving and helps you learn hybrid braking and energy management without being overwhelming.
